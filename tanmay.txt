Simple decorations: Decorate the space with colorful balloons, streamers, and banners to create a festive atmosphere.

Cake smash: A popular tradition for first birthdays is a cake smash photoshoot, where the baby gets their own cake to play with and enjoy.

Special outfit: Dress the baby in a cute and comfortable outfit for the occasion. Many families choose themed outfits or outfits with "1st birthday" written on them.

Family and friends: Invite close family and friends to join in the celebration. Keep the guest list small to ensure the baby feels comfortable and not overwhelmed.

Baby-friendly activities: Plan simple activities that the baby can enjoy, such as playing with toys, listening to music, or engaging in gentle activities like bubbles or peek-a-boo.

Capture memories: Take plenty of photos and videos to capture the precious moments of the day. These memories will be cherished for years to come.

Special gift: Consider giving the baby a meaningful gift that they can enjoy or keep as a keepsake.

Most importantly, enjoy the celebration and the precious moments with the little one. Happy birthday to Tanmay!
